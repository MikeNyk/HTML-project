Here are some swing thoughts to help you play better

  1. Keep your left arm straight during the backswing
    • This will help keep your swing on the correct swing plane
    • This thought can help you create a more repeatable swing
  2. Turn with your chest and hips, not just your arms
    • This can help create a more comforatable swing and help your tempo.
    • This will also help create more power
